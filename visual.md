## Mode change

* linewise visual mode: `V`
* lowercase/uppercase and exit to normal: `u`/`U`
* escape to normal mode: `v`/`<escape>`
* command mode: `:`

## Misc

* record macro: `q`, replay it `@`, e.g. `qa...q` and `@a`
* suspend editor: `<^-z>`
* replace char under cursor: `r`
